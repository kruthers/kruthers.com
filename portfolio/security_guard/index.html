Security Guard 2 - Midnight Hiest

The map

Security Guard 2 is a reimaging of the orginal Security guard Map.
This new version has a masive new map, new relics, better AI and more things todo. This includes new gamemodes new wepons and potions.
The aim of the game is diffrent for the 2 teams. For the Theifs they need to try and steal all the required relics needed to escape, from here depending on the gamemode you may then have to get out of the map via one of the emergency exits!
A guards role is to stop as many relics getting stolen as possible by killing the theifs, both the AI and players! Be warned though the theifs have 2 buff potions at their disposal, 1 will teleport them to a random location around the map, the other will make them invsible. Dont worry though as you have a way to find them, in your hotbar you have a CCTV table that allows to to observe any cameras around the complex, but the theifs can break them to try and hide so keep up your game.



The AI

The ai are villagers that can run around the map steeling relics, there difficault can be changes which changes how quick they move. They too have the 2 bug potions and can use them, as well as this they will run away from you so be allert!

The Map

The Map consits mostly of 2 floors, upper and lower across these each room has a them and there are short cuts the theifs can unlock up stealing certen large relics, be carefull though as the guards can use them too! The relics are all representd by gold blocks on the ground which you shift on to capture!
